# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 22 June 2026_

This app is a reading habit tracker. It is built to be **private by default**.

## Your data stays on your device

Everything you create in the app — your reading days and streaks, your shelves and
books, your reading goals, and your reminder settings — is stored **only on your
device**, in a local database. It is never uploaded to a server, never synced to the
cloud, and never shared with us or anyone else.

We have **no account system and no login**. We do not collect, transmit, or store any
of your personal information. We cannot see your data, because it never leaves your
phone.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

## Permissions we use

- **Camera** — used only to scan a book's barcode so we can look it up for you. Camera
  images are processed on your device and are never stored or transmitted.
- **Notifications** — used only to send the optional daily reading reminder you set up.
  Reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

## Book information

When you search for a book or scan a barcode, the app looks up book details (title,
author, cover) from the [Open Library](https://openlibrary.org/) public API. Only the
search term or barcode number is sent to perform the lookup — **no personal information
about you is included**. See Open Library's
[privacy policy](https://openlibrary.org/privacy) for how they handle requests.

## Analytics and tracking

This app contains **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers**.

## Children's privacy

Because we collect no personal data, the app does not knowingly collect information from
anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published with the app and the
date above will be updated.
